# Video Script Templates

## 1. Insight Video Template

```markdown
[Hook - 3 seconds]
*Pattern interrupt statement*

[Context - 7 seconds]
- Industry relevance
- Current situation

[Core Message - 15 seconds]
- Key insight
- Supporting data
- Expert perspective

[Call to Action - 5 seconds]
- Clear next step
- Value proposition
```

## 2. Process Video Template

```markdown
[Problem - 5 seconds]
*Pain point identification*

[Solution Setup - 5 seconds]
- Quick overview
- Why it works

[Process Steps - 15 seconds]
1. Step one
2. Step two
3. Step three

[Implementation - 5 seconds]
- Quick win
- Resource link
```

## 3. Story Video Template

```markdown
[Situation - 5 seconds]
*Before state*

[Challenge - 10 seconds]
- Main obstacle
- Stakes involved

[Resolution - 10 seconds]
- Solution applied
- Process highlights

[Results - 5 seconds]
- Concrete outcomes
- Lesson learned
```
